What We Do

The Air Traffic Procedures Directorate provides Air Traffic Control (ATC) procedural support to users throughout the National Airspace System. We serve as the principal support group to which all organizations, service areas and field facilities, including Air Route Traffic Control Centers (ARTCCs), Terminal Radar Approach Controls (TRACONs), and ATC Towers, come for guidance on matters involving ATC standards and procedures. Our duties include processing:

  • Document Change Proposals
  • Letters of Authorization
  • Procedural Waivers
  • Procedural Interpretations
  • Notices
  • New/Revised Air Traffic Orders
